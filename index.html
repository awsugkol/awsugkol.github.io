Who We Are

The AWS User Group Kolkata (AWSUGKOL) is an independent, community-led group based in the "City of Joy", India. We are united by our passion for Amazon Web Services.

Our focus spans across Cloud Computing, Serverless Architecture, Scalability, AI/ML (Amazon Bedrock), and Blockchain. Led by a passionate group of volunteers, we aim to bridge the gap between learning and production-scale implementation.
